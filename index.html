The Murat Presents "Solomon's Song Made Real!"
Be Scandalized by a Young Autistic Woman's Take on this Ancient Poem about Sex and Relationship!

The natural candor from her high functioning autism gives her a unique perspective on life. It was this very quality that drew him to her. But, when she freely begins sharing her unadulterated views on sexual pleasure and everything beautiful in a sensual relationship, his religious background screams at him to run for the hills! Even more so when she tells him she got her perspective from the Bible!


Can Solomon's ancient love poem change his mind and break their relationship free from the constraints of the societal and religious thinking which have warped love in America for so long? He'll have to face old wounds and long held fears on the way to the healing and joy they both need.
