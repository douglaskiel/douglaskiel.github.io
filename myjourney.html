Autism

I only learned about this two years ago, and I'm not officially diagnosed, but have seen truths in books of other autistic persons books that confirmed it in my mind, and from my counselors suggestion. The understanding of logic, focused interests and boredom of things that don't interest me have been tale tell signs. Although, I have moved beyond all of this with the help of good friends, loved ones and my faith. The logic and focus of autism are the aspects that will make me very successful in life and in the coding industry.

As quoted from the picture aside: Autism is "The world from a different perspective!" This will and has changed the world.
