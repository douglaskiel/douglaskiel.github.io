Language

Quo usque tandem abutere, Catilina, patientia nostra? This is the first line in one of Marcus Tulius Cicero's, a very pristigious statesman of Rome, most famous oratories. It says, "How long will you abuse our patience, Catiline." Latin was my first look and experience in language and made me the logical person I am today. From Latin, I have learned French, Greek, Korean, some Japanese, and a little Spanish. Now I can add JavaScript to that list of languages that I have learned.
